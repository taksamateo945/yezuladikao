The healthcare sector in Tanzania is expanding rapidly, and with it comes an increased demand for skilled healthcare professionals.

The Ministry of Health, Community Development, Gender, Elderly, and Children in collaboration with the President’s Office Regional Administration and Local Government (PO-RALG) – Tamisemi has announced new employment opportunities for qualified individuals in the healthcare industry.

Ajira Mpya za Afya 2023/2024 Tanzania

The Ajira Mpya za Afya 2023/2024 Tanzania is a government-led initiative aimed at addressing the shortage of healthcare professionals in the country. The government has set aside funds to hire doctors, nurses, laboratory technicians, and other healthcare professionals to improve the quality of healthcare services across the country.

List of healthcare job vacancies announced by the Ministry of Health, Community Development, Gender, Elderly, and Children in collaboration with the President’s Office Regional Administration and Local Government (PO-RALG) – Tamisemi. The vacancies are for different positions, including doctors, nurses, midwives, and laboratory technicians, among others.

The PMO-RALG office They have announced new job opportunities in the health sector, The following is a job advertisement announced:

Ajira za Afya  21,200 2023 Tanzania TAMISEMI

The Priority Health Sector has been allocated 21,200 job vacancies which include: –

21,200vacancies for Hospitals, Health Centers and Dispensaries located in Local Government Authorities. The recruitment process will be overseen by the Office of the President, PMO-RALG;

1,650 vacancies for the recruitment of Health cadre staff in Regional Hospitals, Regional Referral Hospitals; The recruitment process will be overseen by the Ministry of Health; and

1,023 vacancies for Health Colleges, Other Strategic Hospitals and those of District Designated Hospitals (DDHs) and Voluntary Agency (VAHs) contracted by the Government. The recruitment process will be overseen by the Ministry of Health for Health Colleges and other strategic hospitals and the Office of the President, PMO-RALG for DDHs and VAHs Hospitals.
