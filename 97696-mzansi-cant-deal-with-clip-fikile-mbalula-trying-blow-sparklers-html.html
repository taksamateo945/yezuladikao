South African Minister of Transport, Fikile Mbalula, has been a source of entertainment ever since he joined the internet. He is once again making people laugh at his own expense this time it is with a hilarious clip of himself attempting the impossible.

The minister who recently celebrated his 50th birthday was seen in a clip uploaded by SA social media user, @danielmarven attempting to blow out large sparklers that were on his birthday cake - and of course, it was an epic fail that was considerably entertaining to watch.

"Can someone tell him!," @danielmarven captioned the funny clip of Mbaks.

Locals found it just as funny and wasted no time laughing their butts off in the comment section of the post. Read a few of their hilarious comments below:

We previously reported that on Thursday last week, South African transport minister, Fikile Mbalula is celebrating his 50th birthday. In celebration of the minister's special day, many locals have headed online to share some birthday wishes.

The minister himself also took some time out to thank everyone for taking the time to share so many heartwarming well wishes and birthday wishes with him. In the same breath the minister also joked about getting grey hair.

"Good morning Mzansi; thank you for the message of well wishes at my big 50. A small technical Home Affairs error said I was born on the 8th but the 1st is the correct date. At 50 now, you can't make fun of the grey patches on my head."
