As a commerce writer, I see a lot of sales. Like, a lot of sales. But any time Alo Yoga hosts a sale, it’s always something to note. The celeb-loved athleisure brand (celebs like Jennifer Garner, Gigi Hadid, Hailey Bieber, Blake Lively—the list can go on forever—have been spotted wearing Alo), which is known for its stylish and upscale workout gear and iconic yoga pants, rarely puts on a sale. But we’re in luck this week: the retailer is hosting its first-ever, site-wide ‘Alo-versary Sale’ and the time to stock on luxe workout goods is here.

From now through May 14, you can score 20 percent off on dozens of the brand’s top-rated leggings, bras, workout jackets and much more.  We already spotted the High-Waist Biker Shorts for less than $50 and the fan-favorite Alosoft High-Waist 7/8 Highlight Leggings for just $78. Also, this is the perfect chance to explore some of the retailer’s new beauty essentials, like their 60-Second Detox & Glow Mask ($38) (an exfoliating solution that helps to smooth and clear pores). So, if you’re looking to upgrade your athleisure looks and add some gym bag staples in the process, now’s the time to strike.
