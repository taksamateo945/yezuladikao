Who is Ceca:

Ceca is a famous Folk Singer. She was born on June 14, 1973 and her birthplace is Zitorada, Serbia. Early Life and Family:

Before she was famous, Born Svetlana Velickovic, she performed under the stage name of Ceca. She began her highly successful music career in 1988, recording her first album, Cvetak Zanovetak, when she was just fifteen years old. Despite her parents’ disapproval, she married the soon-to-be infamous Serbian military leader and crime boss Zeljko Raznatovic. The marriage, which lasted from 1995 until Raznatovic’s assassination in 2000, resulted in a daughter named Anastasija and a son named Veljko.
