Alfonso Ribeiro, Wife Angela Welcome Baby Boy Anders Reyn

February 11, 2024
blog

A new heir to the Fresh Prince throne! Alfonso Ribeiro and his wife, Angela Unkrich, welcomed their second child together, a son, on Thursday, April 30, at L.A.'s Cedars-Sinai Medical Center, the actor confirms to Us Weekly.

Born at 6:16 a.m., baby Anders Reyn weighed in at 7 pounds, 7 ounces, and measured 19 inches long. He joins big brother Alfonso Jr., 18 months, and half-sister Sienna, 12, Ribeiro's daughter from his marriage to ex-wife Robin Stapler.

"Momma and baby are doing great," the proud third-time dad, 43, tells Us. "The entire family thank you for your warm wishes on this special day!!"
