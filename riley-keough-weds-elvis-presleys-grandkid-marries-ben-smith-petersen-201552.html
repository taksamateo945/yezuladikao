Riley Keough Weds: Elvis Presley's Grandkid Marries Ben Smith-Petersen

August 01, 2024
blog

Burnin' love! Riley Keough married her fiance Ben Smith-Petersen on Wednesday, Feb. 4, Us Weekly can confirm. The Magic Mike actress — and granddaughter of Elvis Presley — said "I do" in Napa, Calif.

Keough, 25, and Smith-Petersen were feted by several famous friends, including Kristen Stewart, Zoe Kravitz, Dakota Johnson, Cara Delevingne, the bride's Mad Max: Fury Road costars Abbey Lee and Courtney Eaton, and Keough's ex-boyfriend Tobias Jesso Jr. Johnson was a bridesmaid for the special ceremony.

Smith-Petersen confirmed back in August that the couple were engaged. "So that happened," the stuntman captioned a Facebook pic of her diamond engagement ring.

The bride and groom first met while filming Mad Max: Fury Road in 2012. "So happy for them. They joked about having a dog 'baby' together now and how it was just a matter of time until they made it official," an insider told Us at the time.
