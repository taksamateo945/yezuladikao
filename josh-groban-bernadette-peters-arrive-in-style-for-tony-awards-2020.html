Josh Groban &amp; Bernadette Peters Arrive in Style for Tony Awards 2020

January 29, 2024
blog

Josh Groban and Bernadette Peters are posing together on the red carpet!

The 40-year-old musician and the 73-year-old theater icon posed together for photos while arriving at the 2020 Tony Awards on Sunday (September 26) at the Winter Garden Theatre in New York City.

PHOTOS: Check out the latest pics of Josh Groban

For the event, Josh looked cool in a navy, velvet tux while Bernadette dazzled in a black gown covered with silver stars.

Following the award show, CBS is airing a two-hour primetime special, The Tony Awards Present: Broadway’s Back!, starting at 9pm ET.

The primetime special will include performances from beloved classics and the three Best Musical nominees.

FYI: Josh is wearing a David ThomasX Jack Victor tux.
